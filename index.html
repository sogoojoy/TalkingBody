TalkingBody

Recent advances in video generation have led to significant improvements in the quality of video generated images as well as motion, but producing realistic character motion effects from a single static portrait is still challenging, and using audio and prompt as control conditions, the existing methods have not yet been applied to generating expressive character expressions, controlling fast and accurate limb movements, and precise voice control. This method proposes TalkingCharacter, which not only solves the above problems, but also has good support for full-body scenes, Chinese scenes, and multi-resolution scenes. The proposed speech injection module uses the cross attn of the window base and combines adaLN and transformer proj to make the speech signal completely and perfectly injected, so that the mouth movement can be precisely controlled under the situation of small face screen. In order to maintain identity consistency, it is proposed to inject high - low dimensional id semantics into the module to ensure the consistency of characters in time and space as well as in difficult angles. Experimental results show that our approach has better mouth accuracy, prompt control, id consistency, and better support in full-body and Chinese scenes.

Generated Videos

TalkingBody can produce highly realistic lip synchronization, ensure that the character's mouth movements match the audio, generate images of various body ranges and directions, and come with natural hand movements. It can also perform emotion control and action control based on prompts, and has stylization capabilities to support the generation of digital humans in full-body scenes.

Realistic Talking Videos

TalkingBody enables the generation of realistic talking videos with various body ranges and orientations, and can generate natural and realistic hand movements that match speech.

Emo & Action Prompt Control

TalkingBody can control the character's emotions (e.g., natural, angry, happy) and actions (making a heart shape with your hands) through prompts

Diverse Character Styles

TalkingBody can animate characters and animals in various styles, generating dynamic, expressive, and naturally realistic stylized videos.
